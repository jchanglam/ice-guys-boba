About Us

We are Ice Guys Boba and Desserts!

We have been proudly serving high-quality teas and desserts since 2010. We were founded by two guys with a dream and passion for boba milk tea. We pride ourselves on serving only the best. We create our drinks with organic tea leaves sourced from local, conflict-free growers. Our syrups are made with the freshest ingredients, no artifical flavorings or preservatives. We love to serve our customers and community. Come visit Ice Guys today!

How We Create Our Drinks

Our drinks are always made-to-order, handmade with love and care!

Meet the Ice Guys

John Doe, Co-founder

John was born and raised in Southern California. He had his first encounter with boba during college and the rest was history. John has a passion for innovation and for serving the community around him.

Michael Lee, Co-founder

Michael spent his childhood in Taiwan and immigrated to the States in middle school. He has a dream of sharing his culture with others and creating drinks that everybody can love. He created the Ice Guys with his best friend John after they both graduated university.
